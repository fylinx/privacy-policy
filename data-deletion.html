Account and Data Deletion

Fylinx: Merge Five Tiles does not require users to create an account. No personal data is collected or stored directly by the developer.

Locally Stored Data

Some game-related data, such as puzzle progress or best scores, may be saved locally on the user's device. This data is not linked to any personal information and can usually be removed by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

Google Play Games Data

If the user chooses to sign in via Google Play Games, certain data such as achievements or leaderboard scores may be stored within the Google Play Games service. These are managed by the user's Google account and not directly accessible to the developer.

Requesting Data Removal

If you have any concerns about your data or wish to request its removal, please contact us at: fylinx.game@gmail.com. We'll respond within a reasonable timeframe and assist you with your request.
